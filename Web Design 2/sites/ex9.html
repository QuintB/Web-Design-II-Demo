Parks as Classrooms



In 1992, NPF launched the National Parks as Classrooms (PAC) education program. Parks as Classrooms developed a model collaboration between San Francisco State University and Golden Gate National Recreation Area. Teachers were provided free curriculum materials, field trip ideas, and workshops for students. The elementary to high school-aged students were able to make connections to real world issues in national parks while also enjoying the outdoors.
