National Parks

There are 58 National Parks in the United States. Find a park near you, or plan a trip to a park in a different state.

State Parks

The United States has more than 10,000 state parks. Find a state park near you to see where to plan your next activity.

Volunteer

The majority of National Park workers are volunteers from around the state. Volunteer benefits include free annual passes for themselves and families.

Classrooms in Parks

Studies show children learn about wildife significantly better when they're outside. Support your local Parks through volunteering or donating today.

Ocean Life

742 National Parks either involve the ocean or are in it. America has and supports a diverse ocean ecosystem on both the Atlantic and Pacific ocean.

Donate

If you don't have the time to volunteer but still want to support our parks, you can donate any amount to the service directly.
